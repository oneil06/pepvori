How physician oversight works

Every patient interaction follows a structured clinical process. No protocol is ever started without physician review.

Intake review

Your health intake is reviewed by a physician within 24 hours. They assess your goals, contraindications, and protocol fit before any prescription is issued.

2
Consultation

You meet with your assigned physician via secure video or async messaging. They answer questions, adjust dosing, and issue your prescription if appropriate.

3
Ongoing check-ins

Your physician monitors progress through your portal check-ins and is available via secure message throughout your protocol cycle.
