Overview

What this protocol
does for you.

The Growth & Strength protocol pairs two of the most clinically validated growth hormone secretagogues — CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin. Together they produce a sustained, pulsatile release of growth hormone that closely mirrors the body's natural pattern, without the side effects associated with synthetic HGH or anabolic steroids.

This protocol is particularly effective for improving lean body mass, reducing visceral fat, and enhancing training performance and recovery. Unlike steroids or exogenous HGH, peptides work by stimulating your own pituitary to produce more GH — meaning the hormonal response is physiological, not pharmacological.

The peptides

What's in

CJC-1295 Modified GRF 1-29

The sustained releaser

Synthetic analogue

CJC-1295 is a synthetic analogue of growth hormone releasing hormone (GHRH). It binds to GHRH receptors in the pituitary and stimulates GH release, but with a significantly extended half-life compared to natural GHRH — meaning GH levels stay elevated for days rather than minutes. This produces a steady baseline increase in GH and IGF-1 that drives the longer-term body composition changes.

Primary action
GH stimulation

Ipamorelin GH Secretagogue

The pulse amplifier

Selective & clean

Ipamorelin is a selective GH secretagogue that works through a different receptor pathway to CJC-1295 — the ghrelin receptor. When used together, they produce a synergistic spike in GH that amplifies the pulsatile release pattern naturally occurring during sleep and exercise. Ipamorelin is particularly valued for its selectivity — it doesn't significantly raise cortisol or prolactin, making it one of the cleanest GH-stimulating peptides available.

What to expect

Your results
timeline.

1
Week 1–2
Sleep quality improves

Most patients notice deeper, more restorative sleep within the first two weeks — an early indicator that GH release is increasing. Energy levels often improve and recovery between training sessions begins to feel faster. This is the priming phase as pituitary sensitivity increases.

2
Week 3–4
Body composition shifts begin

Fat loss — particularly around the midsection — becomes noticeable as elevated GH and IGF-1 increase lipolysis. Muscle fullness and training performance improve. Your physician will conduct a check-in around week 4 to assess progress.

3
Week 5–8
Lean mass accumulation

Muscle protein synthesis is now meaningfully elevated. With consistent training stimulus, lean mass gains accelerate while body fat continues to reduce. Many patients see the most dramatic visual changes in this window.

4
Month 3+
Maintenance & cycling

Your physician will assess whether to cycle off, reduce frequency, or continue on a maintenance dose. Some patients run this protocol continuously; others cycle 3 months on, 1 month off. Bloodwork at this stage can confirm IGF-1 optimisation.
