Overview

What this protocol
does for you.

The Focus & Sleep protocol pairs MK-677 — an oral GH secretagogue — with Selank, a nootropic peptide developed from research into anxiolytic and cognitive-enhancing compounds. Together they target two of the most impactful levers for mental performance: sleep architecture and neural recovery.

Poor sleep and cognitive fog are among the most common complaints in high-performing adults — and among the most poorly addressed by conventional approaches. This protocol works at the biological level, enhancing the restorative quality of sleep rather than just extending its duration, and supporting the neurochemical balance that underlies sustained focus and clarity.

The peptides

What's in

MK-677 Ibutamoren

The deep sleep enhancer

Oral — no injection

MK-677 is an oral GH secretagogue that works by mimicking ghrelin — the hunger hormone — to stimulate GH release through the ghrelin receptor. In the context of this protocol, its most valuable effect is a significant increase in slow-wave (deep) sleep, which is the most restorative sleep stage and the one most responsible for physical repair, memory consolidation, and hormonal regulation. Unlike many sleep aids, it enhances sleep quality without causing dependency or next-day grogginess.

Selank Anxiolytic Nootropic

The cognitive clarifier

Nootropic peptide

Selank is a synthetic heptapeptide derived from tuftsin, an endogenous immunomodulatory peptide. It was originally developed by Russian research institutes as an anxiolytic compound and has an extensive body of research supporting its cognitive-enhancing effects. It works by modulating GABA receptors, increasing BDNF (brain-derived neurotrophic factor), and stabilising serotonin and dopamine metabolism — producing a calm, focused state without sedation or dependency.
