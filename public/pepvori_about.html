The science

Peptides by
the numbers.

The clinical evidence behind peptide therapy is growing rapidly — here's what the research shows.

$50B+
Global peptide therapeutics market

The peptide therapeutics market exceeded $50 billion in 2023 and is projected to grow at over 9% annually through 2030, driven by demand for targeted, low-side-effect therapies.

7,000+
Known naturally occurring peptides

The human body produces over 7,000 known peptides — signalling molecules that regulate everything from tissue repair and immune function to hormonal balance and cognitive performance.

100+
FDA-approved peptide drugs

More than 100 peptide-based drugs have received FDA approval, with over 400 more in active clinical development — making peptides one of the fastest-growing therapeutic classes in modern medicine.

14%
GH decline per decade after 30

Growth hormone declines by approximately 14% per decade beginning in the third decade of life — driving increases in visceral fat, reductions in lean mass, and deteriorating sleep quality.

2–4 wks
Average onset for BPC-157

Multiple preclinical and clinical studies demonstrate that BPC-157 — the cornerstone of the Recovery & Repair protocol — produces measurable tissue repair within 2 to 4 weeks of treatment.

30min
Average half-life of natural GHRH

Natural growth hormone releasing hormone (GHRH) has a half-life of under 30 minutes. Synthetic analogues like CJC-1295 extend this to several days — enabling sustained, physiological GH elevation.

What we stand for

The principles behind
everything we build.

Physician-first, always

Every protocol is prescribed by a licensed, board-certified physician who reviews your individual health profile. No algorithmic prescribing, no shortcuts. The physician has full discretion over every clinical decision.

Radical transparency

Compounded peptides are not FDA-approved finished drug products — we say this clearly and often. We tell patients exactly what they're taking, why, what the evidence shows, and what to expect. No overpromising.

Built for the patient

No waiting rooms. No repeat GP visits. No chasing referrals. Everything — intake, consultation, prescription, fulfilment, follow-up — is handled online, on your schedule, and built around your goals.
